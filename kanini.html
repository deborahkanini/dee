Capabilities

One

Separate legal entity: One of the most important features of a company is that it is a separate legal entity. It means that the company is distinct from its owners or shareholders. The company has its own legal identity, and it can enter into contracts, own property, and sue or be sued in its own name

Two

Limited liability: Another important feature of a company is limited liability. This means that the liability of the shareholders is limited to the amount of their investment in the company. In case the company incurs losses or faces legal action, the shareholders are not personally liable beyond their investment in the company.

Three

Perpetual succession: A company has perpetual succession, which means that it continues to exist even if its shareholders change. The company has a separate legal existence, and its existence is not affected by the death, retirement, or insolvency of its shareholders.
